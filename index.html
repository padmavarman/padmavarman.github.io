about

A little about me.

I've spent the last 6+ years working closely with Product and Sales teams, helping them make better decisions with data. Right now I'm at HSBC in New York, where I dig into large datasets to find opportunities that nobody's looked at yet. One of my favorite moments was uncovering $5M in untapped revenue from a dataset that had been sitting there for months.

I work mostly with SQL, Python, Tableau, Power BI, and cloud tools like BigQuery and AWS. But honestly, the tools are just tools. What I really care about is making sure the insights actually land with the people who need them, whether that's a VP in a boardroom or a new analyst learning the ropes. I've built dashboards, automated messy reporting pipelines, and trained teams to be more self-sufficient with data.
